Punk
Newspaper, 2025
Special Thanks to Brad Bartlett

This project focuses on British punk culture, examining its social background, unemployed youth, key figures, DIY ethos, and the role of zines as tools for expression and distribution. It also traces punk's conceptual connections to Dada and the Situationist International, highlighting its critical stance toward authority and mass culture. Rather than adopting a chaotic visual language, the project uses a relatively rational typographic system to frame punk as an intentional cultural and political movement. Pink is introduced as a deliberate color choice, reflecting punk's history of provocation, contradiction, and resistance to fixed meanings.
