Brand Identiy Design, 2024

This project explores the evolving relationship between translated texts and book cover design, proposing a cohesive visual system for contemporary readers and collectors. By examining how translation reshapes cultural context, the project positions book covers as adaptive visual objects rather than static identities.

The branding system was applied to three translated novels in Chinese, French, and Japanese, each reflecting its cultural and linguistic characteristics while maintaining overall coherence.

The books are designed in a compact, portable format (105 × 150 mm), emphasizing accessibility and everyday reading, and reinforcing the idea of translated literature as something lived with, carried, and revisited.
