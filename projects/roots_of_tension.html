Roots of Tension
Game Design, 2024

When two plants are placed side by side, one may wither and die—suggesting that competition exists even among plants. The gases they emit function like invisible emotional bubbles, subtly influencing nearby plants and the balance of the environment.

This idea becomes a workplace-themed game where plants act as protagonists. Each plant emits emotions such as stress or pride, shaping both its own growth and the surrounding space.

Bold posters and pixelated visuals place characters against rivals, turning invisible competition into something interactive and tangible. Retro aesthetics contrast with the serious theme of workplace pressure, creating a narrative of growth and survival.

The game takes place across different workspace gardens, each with its own atmosphere and challenges. Players interact with these environments by selecting actions or tools—such as working or relaxing—while navigating NPCs that emit emotional influences.

As players progress, new options and scenes are unlocked, allowing for deeper interaction and personalization. Player choices continuously affect the emotional states of NPCs, gradually reshaping the environment and determining how each scene evolves.
