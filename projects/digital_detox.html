Digital Detox
Exhibition Design, 2025
Special Thanks to Tyrone Drake

Digital Detox is an immersive typographic narrative installation inspired by Vaporwave aesthetics and digital culture. Through glitchy visuals, nostalgic icons, and interactive typography, the installation guides audiences through a journey, from seamless digital comfort to alienation and critical awakening. By transforming familiar childhood memories and digital symbols into physical experiences, the project encourages reflection on surveillance, consumerism, and the emotional impact of life inside algorithm-driven systems.

Mabel Chen © 2026
